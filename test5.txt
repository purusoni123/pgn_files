The statutory objectives of the FPC are to contribute to the achievement of the Bank’s Financial Stability Objective and, subject to that, to support the economic policy of the Government, including its objectives for growth and employment. The FPC has powers to give Directions, through the setting of specified macroprudential instruments, to the PRA and the Financial Conduct Authority. It can make Recommendations to any body, including: the PRA and Financial Conduct Authority on a ‘comply or explain’ basis where appropriate; and to HM Treasury, including on the boundaries within and around the perimeter of the regulatory regime.
